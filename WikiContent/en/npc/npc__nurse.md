## Yu Zhi: Nurse

### Her role
- Yu Zhi handles healing and relationship progression.
- She gives the mod a support and recovery layer beyond pure combat.

### Why players should care
- Healing services matter during heavy challenge cycles.
- **Calming Drops** are tied to her line and are practical, not just thematic.
- She is also part of the marriage system path.

[tip] Do not only visit Yu Zhi when you are already desperate. She is part of the mod's long-term structure.
