## Dingdang: Goblin Smith

### His role
- Dingdang is the reforge specialist.
- If you want to move from "usable loot" to "a real build piece", he matters.

### Main function
- Reforge equipment.
- Spend more money for better quality outcomes.
- Use **Cold Quench Fluid** to lock valuable stats.

### Key related items
- **Diamond** for positive interaction.
- **Brick Stone** for negative interaction.
- **Dingdang Drawing** as a special gift-related item.

[tip] Reforge after you have a real candidate item. Do not waste resources rolling everything you pick up.
