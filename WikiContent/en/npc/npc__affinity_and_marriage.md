## Bonds, Gifts and Marriage

### Bond progression
- NPC affinity normally progresses from **level 1 to 10**.
- Dialogue, gifts, and certain item interactions can raise or lower it.
- The system includes **daily decay**, so long-term neglect has a cost.

### Gift system basics
- Different NPCs react differently to the same item.
- Some items are clearly positive gifts.
- Some items trigger negative reactions.
- Gifts go through a dedicated gift-container interaction flow, not a loose roleplay prompt.

### Key relationship items
- **Diamond**
- **Brick Stone**
- **Peace Charm**
- **Calming Drops**
- **Dingdang Drawing**
- **Diamond Ring**

### Marriage rules
- **Diamond Ring** is the key proposal item.
- The target NPC must reach **affinity level 10**.
- You must also build the **Wedding Chapel** first, or the proposal will be rejected.
- A successful proposal triggers a marriage cutscene, using video if available and dialogue fallback otherwise.

### Important restrictions
- Being married does not remove consequences.
- Giving rings to other NPCs after marriage can trigger penalties.
- Re-gifting rings to the same spouse also follows special rejection logic.

[tip] If you want to understand the mod completely, the relationship system is not optional reading. It is real content.
