## Awen (Courier)

### Overview

Awen is a Courier NPC who provides item storage and retrieval services. He appears in normal maps and also within all BossRush modes, allowing you to store and retrieve items at any time during combat.

### Services
- Courier Service: Move items into the courier box and pay Awen's delivery fee to ship them home
- `Store All`: Move root items from your inventory into the courier box in one action, while skipping locked slots
- Storage Service: Store items directly into the Storage Point, saved across scenes
- `Deposit All`: Send eligible root items from your inventory straight into the Storage Point, while skipping locked slots
- Item Retrieval: Retrieve previously stored items from the Storage Point

### Storage Point Mechanics
- Storage Point data is persistently saved and will not be lost when exiting the game
- Stored items can be retrieved in any scene where Awen appears
- The Storage Point serves as a bridge for transferring items between different modes

#### Integration with Blood Hunt

When you successfully extract in Blood Hunt mode, bounty reward items from the shared high-quality reward pool are automatically sent to the Storage Point. Return to normal maps and find Awen to retrieve these rewards after extraction.

#### Awen Courier Token

- The base merchant sells the `Awen Courier Token`
- Using it ships your currently equipped items, backpack items, and pet inventory home in one action
- It is free to use in base; elsewhere it costs the same as Awen's normal courier service

### Tips
- Awen has no Affinity system and does not require daily interaction; just find him whenever you need to store or retrieve items
- Before entering high-risk modes, you can store valuable items with Awen to avoid accidental loss
- When you need to clear inventory quickly, use `Store All` / `Deposit All`; locked slots are now left untouched
- Blood Hunt extraction rewards are automatically sent to the Storage Point, and currently come from the shared high-quality reward pool, so remember to pick them up after extraction
- Awen is present within all BossRush modes, so you can store and retrieve equipment at any time
