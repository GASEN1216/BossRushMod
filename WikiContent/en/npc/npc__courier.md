## Awen: Courier

### His role
- Awen is one of the first important NPCs for new players.
- He gives you the **Adventurer's Journal**, which is the in-game wiki item.
- He also handles storage-oriented utility.

### Why players should care
- He reduces item-management pain.
- He helps long-term play feel structured instead of messy.
- He is part of how this mod becomes a full progression experience instead of isolated boss runs.

[tip] Do not treat Awen as a one-time tutorial NPC. His utility becomes more valuable the longer you play.
