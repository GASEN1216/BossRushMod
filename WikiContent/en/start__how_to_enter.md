## How to Enter BossRush

### Standard entry
- The normal entry item is the **Boss Rush Ticket**.
- Bring the ticket, use the proper entry interaction, and enter a standard BossRush run.

### Mode D entry
- **Mode D: Rags to Riches** requires:
- **Naked entry**
- **Boss Rush Ticket**
- If you bring regular gear, the mode will not start as intended.

### Mode E entry
- **Mode E: Faction War** requires:
- **Naked entry**
- **A faction flag**
- Flags are not flavor items. They are the real access key for the mode.

### Before your first run
- Make sure you know which mode you are trying to start.
- Ticket-based entry and flag-based entry are not interchangeable.
- If a mode fails to start, check your gear first. Naked-entry rules are strict.

[warn] If you are wearing equipment, Mode D and Mode E can reject the run even if you brought the correct item.
