## Reforge and Achievements

### Reforge
- Reforging is about controlling growth on important equipment.
- You choose a piece, invest money, and reroll for better outcomes.
- **Cold Quench Fluid** is what lets you protect valuable stats instead of gambling everything every time.

### Achievements
- Achievement categories cover:
- **Basic clears**
- **Flawless clears**
- **Speedruns**
- **Boss kills**
- **Cumulative goals**
- **Special challenges**
- **Ultimate completion**

### Why it matters
- Achievements are one of the mod's major long-term structures.
- They provide rewards and make repeated play feel directed instead of random.

[tip] Start with clear and kill goals first. Leave hidden extremes and hard flawless runs for later.
