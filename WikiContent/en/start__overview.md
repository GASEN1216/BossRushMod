## Mod Overview

### What Is BossRush Mod?

A massive content expansion for Escape from Duckov (Steam Workshop). What started as a Boss arena grew into a full content pack: 5 modes, 3 custom Bosses, NPC companions, gear progression, and an achievement system.

### At a Glance

- **5 Game Modes** — Standard, Infinite Hell, From Scratch, Faction War, Blood Hunt
- **9 Maps** — Covering most in-game scenes
- **3 Custom Bosses** — Dragon Descendant, Skyburner Dragon Lord, Phantom Witch (unique skills + exclusive drops)
- **3 NPCs** — Dingdang (Smith), Yuori (Nurse), Awen (Courier)
- **9 Custom Equipment Pieces** — Dragon Set, Dragon King Set, Cloud Rider, Reverse Scale, Halberd, Dragon Breath, Dragon Cannon, Frostmourne, Soulreaper's Requiem
- **20+ Items** — Consumables, mode-exclusive props, functional items
- **Base Buildings** — Wedding Chapel, Dust-Covered StarWish Fountain
- **Reforge System** — Stat rerolling + Cold Quench Fluid to lock affixes
- **Affinity & Marriage** — NPC relationship progression, gifting, weddings
- **35 Achievements** — Completion, no-hit, speedrun, collection, and more
- **In-Game Encyclopedia** — Adventurer's Journal item
- **Config System** — Wave intervals, Boss count, difficulty multipliers, etc.

### Languages

Full Chinese + English support: items, NPC dialogue, UI, encyclopedia.

### Compatibility

- Steam Workshop subscription — one click install
- No original game files modified (Mod loader + Harmony patches)
- Stays compatible with game updates
