## Mode E: Faction War

### Entry condition
- **Naked entry plus a faction flag**.

### What this mode actually is
- This is not normal BossRush with a faction skin.
- It is a multi-faction sandbox battle mode.
- You choose a faction, or enter with a random flag and let the game decide.

### Core rules
- Allies in the same faction do not damage each other.
- Different factions fight each other.
- The more deaths a faction suffers, the stronger its remaining members become.
- Special faction items can reshape the battlefield on demand.

### Why players like it
- Large emergent battles.
- Opportunity to let factions weaken each other first.
- Strong sandbox tools through flags and Mode E items.
- A special solo path through the **Lone Wolf Flag**.

### First-run advice
- Do not start with the Lone Wolf route.
- Learn faction flow, spawn patterns, and map pressure first.

[warn] Mode E becomes chaotic very quickly. If you use its special items without control, the map can turn against you in seconds.
