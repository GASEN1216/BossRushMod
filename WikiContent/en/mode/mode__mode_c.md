## Infinite Hell

### What Is It?

No end. No evacuation. Bosses get +2% stronger every wave, forever. **How long can you last?**

### Entry

- **BossRush Ticket** + select "Infinite Hell" at the arena signpost

### Core Rules

- **Bosses/wave**: 3 (adjustable in config)
- **Waves**: Unlimited
- **Boss scaling**: +2% HP and damage per wave (cumulative)
- **Loot crates**: None — uses a cash pool instead
- **Clear condition**: Can't clear. Survive until you don't

### Cash Pool & Rewards

No loot crates. Instead, every Boss kill adds cash to a pool displayed on the signpost. Cash pickups within 2m auto-fly to you; farther out, you need to walk over.

#### Milestone Rewards

- **Every 5 waves** — 1 high-quality random item (prefers Q5+ and value ≥ 10,000)
- **Every 100 waves** — Grand prize: Crown(s) + massive cash reward (exponential growth)

#### 100-Wave Milestones

- Wave 100: 1 Crown + 10M cash
- Wave 200: 2 Crowns + 20M cash
- Wave 300: 4 Crowns + 40M cash
- Wave 400: 8 Crowns + 80M cash
- Pattern: Crowns ×2, Cash ×2 each time. **The longer you last, the richer you get.**

### Signpost Features

In Infinite Hell, the signpost also shows:
- Ammo resupply shop
- Current cash pool total
- Current wave number

### Achievements

- **10 waves** → Infinite Hell · Ten (50K) / **25** → Twenty-Five (150K) / **50** → Fifty (350K)
- **100** → Hundred (800K) / **200** → Two Hundred (2M)
- **10 waves no damage** → Iron Will (500K)
- **10 waves no healing** → Iron Man Challenge (hidden, 250K)

### Tips

- +2% per wave sounds small. At wave 50 it's +100%. At wave 100 it's +200%. **It adds up fast.**
- No loot crates = bring your best gear in, or rely on milestone rewards
- Boss Filter lets you tune the pool — remove the Bosses you hate most
