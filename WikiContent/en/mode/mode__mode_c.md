## Mode C: Infinite Hell

### Core rules
- Endless wave progression.
- Bosses per wave can be configured.
- This is the long-form mode for money, endurance, achievements, and boss-pool tuning.

### What makes it special
- It is built for sustained runs, not short clears.
- Cash auto-absorb helps reduce pickup friction during long fights.
- Boss filter and boss-specific factor tuning matter much more here than in short modes.

### Best use cases
- Farming money.
- Grinding long-term achievement goals.
- Practicing specific boss sets over time.
- Stress-testing late-game builds.

### What usually kills players here
- Not one boss.
- Poor field management, bad pacing, bad healing habits, and letting lootboxes ruin the arena.

[warn] Do not raise both bosses-per-wave and global boss multiplier too early. That hides mechanics instead of teaching them.
