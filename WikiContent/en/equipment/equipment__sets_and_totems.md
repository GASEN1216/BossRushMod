## Sets, Totems and Reverse Scale

### Dragon Set
- Built from **Crimson Dragon Helm** and **Flame Scale Armor**.
- Core benefits:
- **Double-tap directional dash**
- **Fire damage converted into healing**

### Dragon King Set
- Built from **Dragon King's Crown** and **Dragon King's Scale Mail**.
- This is the stronger dragon progression path.
- Core benefits:
- **Stronger Dragon King dash**
- **Chain dash follow-up**
- **Lava trail burn**
- **Fire damage converted into healing**

### Cloud Soar I
- One of the clearest build-changing items in the mod.
- It gives real flight and glide-style mobility.
- It is useful for big maps, boss evasion, vertical movement, and Mode E battlefield control.

### Reverse Scale
- A very strong emergency totem.
- When it triggers near death, it restores health, fires prismatic bolts, and then breaks.
- It is a last-chance swing tool, not a passive always-on stat line.

[tip] Sets and totems are not competing for the same job. They push your build in different directions.
