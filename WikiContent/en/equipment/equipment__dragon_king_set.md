## Dragon King Set

### Overview
- The Dragon King Set is the advanced version of the Dragon Set, dropped by Skyburner Dragon Lord
- Retains the fire-to-heal core passive, greatly enhances dash ability, and removes poison vulnerability and field of view reduction
- Once obtained, it is a full upgrade — directly replace the Dragon Set

### Set Pieces

#### Dragon King Crown (Helmet)
- Armor: 7
- Durability: 200
- Physical Resistance: -15%
- Storm Resistance: +1
- Cold Resistance: +1
- Critical Damage: +15%
- Fire Resistance: -20%
- Shock Resistance: -20%
- Drop Source: Skyburner Dragon Lord (15% drop rate)
- Compared to Crimson Dragon Helm: Higher Critical Damage (+15% vs +10%), removed poison vulnerability and field of view reduction

#### Dragon King Scale Armor (Armor)
- Armor: 7
- Durability: 200
- Physical Resistance: -25%
- Storm Resistance: +1
- Cold Resistance: +1
- Fire Resistance: -25%
- Shock Resistance: -25%
- Drop Source: Skyburner Dragon Lord (15% drop rate)
- Compared to Flame Scale Armor: Removed poison vulnerability

### Set Effect (Both Pieces Equipped)
- Fire-to-heal: Fire damage no longer hurts you; instead it restores 80% of the equivalent amount as health
- Dragon Eye glow effect: A red breathing light effect appears on the head
- Dragon King dash: First dash covers 6 meters, 0.5-second cooldown
- Second dash: Press a direction key within 0.15 seconds after the first dash to chain a 3-meter second dash
- Magma trail: The dash path leaves a magma zone that deals continuous damage to enemies (5 damage per 0.5 seconds, lasts 3 seconds)
- Golden-orange afterimage: Dash leaves a golden-orange afterimage (distinct from the Dragon Set's orange-red)

### Comparison with Dragon Set
- Dash distance: 3 meters → 6 meters + 3-meter second dash
- Dash cooldown: 1.5 seconds → 0.5 seconds
- Magma trail: None → Yes (damages enemies)
- Poison vulnerability: Yes → No
- Field of view reduction: Yes → No
- Critical Damage: +10% → +15%

[tip] The Dragon King Set is a full upgrade; once obtained, it is recommended to directly replace the Dragon Set.
