## Dragon King Weapons

### Dragon's Breath
- **Dragon's Breath** is a signature dragon-line firearm.
- It is not just a recolored normal gun.
- It gives the dragon route a real ranged identity.

### Inferno Emperor's Realm-Breaking Halberd
- This is the flagship Dragon King melee weapon.
- Its identity comes from:
- **A full combo structure**
- **A dedicated right-click skill**
- **Dragon Flame Mark stacking and detonation**

### Choosing between them
- Use Dragon's Breath if you want steadier ranged control.
- Use the halberd if you want aggressive close pressure, combo rhythm, and explosive melee payoff.
