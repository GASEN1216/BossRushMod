## Frostmourne

### Overview
- An ice-element exclusive melee weapon with its own icy aura visuals
- Right click casts `Undead Summoning`, calling 5 undead servants to fight around you
- One of the few custom weapons that can be obtained as an extra drop from the vanilla `Cname_Boss_Blue`

### Basic Info
- Type: Melee weapon
- Quality: 6
- Element: Ice
- Drop Source: Every `Cname_Boss_Blue` instance has a 50% extra-drop chance on kill
- Death Drop: Will not drop from player equipment slots on death

### Weapon Stats
- Base Damage: 38.5
- Attack Speed: 1.54
- Attack Range: 2.0m
- Crit Rate: 7%
- Crit Damage: 1.4x
- Armor Piercing: 4.2
- Bleed Chance: 10.5%
- Block Bullet: 0.7
- Stamina Cost: 7 per hit
- Move Speed: 107%
- Deal Damage Time: 0.084 sec
- Cold Protection: +2

### Frost Power
- All melee attacks are converted to ice-element damage
- Hits apply the vanilla `Cold` effect
- The weapon also grants `Cold Protection +2`

### Undead Summoning (Right Click)
- Cooldown: 10 seconds
- Stamina Cost: 14
- Summon Count: 5
- Summon Radius: 2.5m
- Undead Health: 100
- Summons join the player's faction and do not spawn loot crates on death

### Usage Tips
- More stable and general-purpose than Skyburner Halberd, trading combo burst for steady melee performance
- The right-click summon is especially useful in BossRush or high-pressure fights to split aggro and add safety
- Its ice damage plus cold protection makes it broadly useful in snow maps and debuff-heavy situations

[tip] If Frostmourne appears in a Blue Boss loot crate, it now follows the same search flow as the other items in that crate.
