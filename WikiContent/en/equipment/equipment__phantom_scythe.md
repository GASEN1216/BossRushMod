## Soulreaper's Requiem

### Overview
- An exclusive melee weapon dropped by the Phantom Witch, a Ghost-element scythe
- Normal attacks have a 50% chance to apply a Curse slow; right-click deploys a Curse Realm

### Basic Info
- Type: Melee weapon
- Quality: 6 (Epic)
- Element: Ghost
- Drop source: Phantom Witch (50% extra drop chance)
- Death drop: Will not drop on death

### Weapon Stats
- Base Damage: 35.5
- Attack Speed: 1.56
- Attack Range: 2.22m
- Crit Rate: 5.5%
- Crit Damage: 1.33x
- Armor Piercing: 3.8
- Bleed Chance: 8%
- Block Bullet: 0.45
- Stamina Cost: 6.5 per hit
- Move Speed: 120%
- Deal Damage Time: 0.09 sec
- Gem Slots: 2

### Soulreaving Power (Passive)
- All normal attacks deal Ghost-element damage
- 50% chance to apply Curse debuff on hit
- Curse effect: -30% move speed per stack, up to 3 stacks, lasts 5 seconds

### Requiem Realm (Right Click)
- Cooldown: 12 seconds
- Stamina Cost: 20
- Windup: 0.25 seconds
- Unfurls a violet rune circle beneath you
- Realm radius: 4m
- Realm duration: 4 seconds
- Deals 22 Ghost damage every 0.5s to enemies inside and applies Curse

### Usage Tips
- High move speed (120%) enables agile repositioning and kiting
- Normal-attack Curse stacks effectively slow enemies; at 3 stacks they are nearly immobilized
- Right-click Realm is ideal for holding chokepoints or zoning; standing inside lets you slow and damage surrounding enemies simultaneously
- Raw damage is slightly below Frostmourne, but the mobility and crowd-control advantages are stronger

[tip] This weapon's core strength lies in mobility and control, not raw damage numbers.
