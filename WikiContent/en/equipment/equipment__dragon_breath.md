## Dragon Breath

### Overview
- An exclusive firearm dropped by Dragon Descendant, the first custom weapon you can obtain
- Has a chance to inflict Dragon Flame Burn on hit, dealing continuous true damage

### Basic Info
- Type: Firearm
- Drop Source: Dragon Descendant (10% drop rate)
- Death Drop: Drops normally

### Special Effects
- Dragon Flame Burn: 50% chance per hit to inflict a burn debuff
- Burn damage: 1 true damage per tick per stack (ignores Armor)
- Stackable: Multiple triggers can stack multiple layers of burn, accumulating damage over time

### Usage Tips
- The core value lies in sustained burn true damage, ideal against high-Armor targets
- The 50% trigger rate means high fire rate can quickly stack multiple burn layers
- Pair with the Dragon Set for a complete Dragon Descendant equipment combo

### Related Achievements
- Dragon Descendant Collection — Collect all exclusive drops from Dragon Descendant (Crimson Dragon Helm, Flame Scale Armor, Dragon Breath)

[tip] Dragon Breath is your entry point into the custom equipment system — don't underestimate its burn stacking.
