## Mode E Strategy

### First principle
- Mode E is a battlefield simulator, not normal BossRush.

### Common approaches
- **Faction survival**: fight with your side.
- **Opportunist play**: let other factions weaken each other first.
- **Lone Wolf challenge**: enter with the Lone Wolf Flag and fight the whole map alone.

### Biggest beginner mistakes
- Standing in the center of every faction's line of fire.
- Wasting Mode E tools without a plan.
- Chasing one target while the battlefield collapses around you.

[tip] The strongest weapon in Mode E is battlefield control, not raw DPS.
