## New Player Route

### Suggested sequence
- Get the **Adventurer's Journal**.
- Learn standard entry with the **Boss Rush Ticket**.
- Clear **Mode A** first.
- Stabilize in **Mode B**.
- Then explore NPCs, drops, reforging, and achievements.
- Move into **Infinite Hell**, **Mode D**, and **Mode E** after that.

[tip] The goal of your first hours is understanding structure, not forcing the hardest mode immediately.
