## Infinite Hell and Mode D

### Infinite Hell advice
- Decide whether you want money, achievement progress, drop farming, or pure endurance.
- Manage the arena, not just the bosses.
- Boss filter and boss-factor tuning matter a lot here.

### Mode D advice
- This is a growth run, not a comfort run.
- Use what you get.
- Live through weak starts instead of waiting for a fantasy setup.

[warn] Many Mode D failures come from standard BossRush habits. You cannot play "from nothing" like you already have a finished build.
