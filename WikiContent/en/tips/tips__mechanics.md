## Challenge Mechanics

### Key Mechanics
- All boss mechanics in this mod can be learned from **Courier Awen**
- Boss spawn points are at the edges of the arena, be careful when walking before spawn locations are determined

![Mechanics-BossSpawnPoints](TODO.png)

### Wave Flow
- After defeating each wave, you can choose to continue at the center
- Default wave interval is about **15 seconds** (configurable)

![Mechanics-WaveIntervalAndContinuePrompt](TODO.png)

### Loot & Cover
- If loot randomization is enabled, boss loot boxes can be **picked up and placed**
- Whether loot boxes can **block bullets** (act as cover) is configurable

![Mechanics-LootBoxAndCover](TODO.png)

### Other
- Supports custom bosses from all other mods
- You can leave anytime from the evacuation point on the right
- [warn]Death drops all your items[/warn]
- [tip]Victory rewards are equally generous![/tip]
