## Blood Hunt Guide

### Core Concept
- Blood Hunt is the hardest mode in the Mod.
- You continuously lose health, and the only way to heal is by killing Bosses.
- Health drain accelerates across four phases, with the ultimate goal of successfully extracting during the extraction phase.

### Preparation Phase (180s, 1%/sec)
- The most relaxed phase with the lowest drain rate.
- Kill a few Bosses quickly to heal and obtain fortification items.
- Scavenge gear and upgrade weapons and armor from loot crates.
- Pre-deploy covers at key positions.
- Familiarize yourself with the map layout and memorize spawn point locations.

### Bounty Phase (180s, 1.5%/sec)
- The bounty list is generated — all surviving Bosses are marked.
- Watch the Bounty Radar and prioritize killing high-mark bounty Bosses.
- Killing bounty Bosses heals more (75% vs 50%) and grants extra drops.
- Bosses also kill each other and inherit Bounty Marks — keep an eye on changes to the Bounty Leader.
- Accumulated Bounty Marks = number of rewards upon extraction.

### Hunt Surge Phase (180s, 2%/sec)
- All Bosses begin an all-out hunt for you.
- Unmarked Bosses gain +50% movement speed — very fast.
- This is the most dangerous combat phase; you must maintain a high kill pace to sustain your health.
- Fortifications are critical in this phase — use covers and roadblocks to create safe firing positions.
- Don't stop — keep moving and fighting.

### Extraction Phase (unlimited, 3%/sec)
- The extraction point spawns far from your location.
- All Bosses gain +50% movement speed; unmarked Bosses gain +100%.
- Drain rate is at its highest — 3% of initial max health per second.
- Don't linger in fights — head straight for the extraction point.
- Stand at the extraction point for 15 seconds to successfully extract.
- You can deploy fortifications to protect yourself during extraction.

[warn] At 3% health drain per second during extraction, hesitation means death.

### Fortification Usage Strategy
- **Foldable Cover Pack**: Deploy anytime for temporary cover; you get the most of these.
- **Reinforced Roadblock Pack**: Deploy at key chokepoints or around the extraction point; 5000 HP makes them very durable.
- **Barbed Wire Pack**: Deploy on Boss approach routes to slow their advance.
- **Emergency Repair Spray**: Repair damaged fortifications to extend their lifespan.

### Extraction Point Defense
- After reaching the extraction point, quickly deploy fortifications around it.
- Use Reinforced Roadblock Packs to block the main approaches.
- Barbed Wire slows approaching Bosses.
- Foldable Covers fill in the gaps.
- Use Emergency Repair Spray to maintain damaged fortifications.
- Hold for 15 seconds to complete extraction.

### Bounty Mark Strategy
- The more Bounty Marks you have, the richer the extraction rewards.
- But pursuing marks means more combat risk.
- Balance survival and profit: making it out alive matters more than grabbing a few extra marks.
- Killing high-mark Bosses heals more (75% + max health increase per mark count).

### Recommended Gear
- High DPS weapons: You need fast Boss kills to sustain your health.
- Dragon King Set: Dash mobility + fire immunity.
- Cloud Rider totem: Aerial mobility for quickly reaching the extraction point.
- Reverse Scale: A last-resort lifesaver.
- Plenty of ammo: Can be purchased from the Mystery Merchant.

[tip] The most essential skill in Blood Hunt is pacing — knowing when to fight, when to run, and when to deploy fortifications.
