## Waves, Loot and Rewards

### Standard reward loop
- Wave progression is the main structure.
- Lootboxes are the main visible reward containers.
- Difficulty, pacing, and field control all affect how much value you actually extract.

### Lootboxes are part of combat flow
- They hold rewards.
- They can also block bullets if that option is enabled.
- Too many boxes can ruin movement and visibility, which is why cleanup tools matter.

### Infinite Hell reward experience
- Infinite Hell is designed for long runs.
- **Cash auto-absorb** helps reduce pickup friction during sustained combat.
- That makes endurance farming feel smoother and more intentional.

### Why cleanup matters
- Clear all lootboxes when the arena is getting clogged.
- Clear only empty lootboxes when you still want to preserve active rewards.
- Good arena hygiene matters more than players expect in long sessions.
