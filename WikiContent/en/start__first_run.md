## First Run Guide

### Recommended order
- Start with **Mode A: Easy**.
- Then move to **Mode B: Standard**.
- After that, begin exploring NPCs, boss drops, and gear systems.
- Only then move into **Infinite Hell**, **Mode D**, and **Mode E**.

### What to pay attention to
- How the **signpost** works.
- How wave flow feels between fights.
- How lootboxes pile up and affect movement.
- Which bosses already give you trouble.
- Which items are clearly system items, not vendor trash.

### What new players often miss
- The in-game wiki matters.
- NPCs are not background decoration.
- Reforging, achievements, and relationship systems are part of the real content, not side notes.

[tip] Your first goal is not a perfect run. It is building a correct mental model of the mod.
