## Boss Overview

### What the boss pool includes
- BossRush uses the available boss preset pool and layers custom bosses on top.
- Standard modes use wave rules.
- Mode E adds faction-based spawning and battlefield interaction between enemy groups.

### The two key custom bosses
- **Dragon Descendant**
- **Skyburner Dragon Lord**

### Why the boss pool matters
- You can enable or disable bosses through the Boss Filter.
- Infinite Hell can also adjust boss-specific spawn factors.
- That means you can turn BossRush into a practice arena, a farming route, or a focused dragon hunt.

[tip] If you want a specific drop line, check your boss filter first. Do not accidentally remove your own target.
