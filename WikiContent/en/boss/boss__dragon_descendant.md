## Dragon Descendant

### What this boss represents
- The Dragon Descendant is more than a one-off enemy.
- It is the gateway into the dragon-themed gear and weapon line.
- If you start chasing dragon gear seriously, this boss becomes important very quickly.

### Why players fight it
- It is tied to the early dragon gear route.
- It points you toward the Dragon set and the Dragon's Breath weapon path.

### Practical advice
- Learn it on an open map first.
- Focus on motion reading and safe windows instead of brute-force trading.
- If a map makes the fight feel unreadable, change the map before changing everything else.

[tip] Think of the Dragon Descendant as the front door to the mod's dragon progression, not the final destination.
