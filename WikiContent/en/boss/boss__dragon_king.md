## Skyburner Dragon Lord

### What this boss represents
- This is one of the mod's signature high-end custom bosses.
- It sits at the center of the Dragon King progression line.
- If you want the strongest dragon-themed endgame rewards, you will eventually have to beat it consistently.

### Why players farm it
- Its reward line is tied to:
- **Dragon King's Crown**
- **Dragon King's Scale Mail**
- **Cloud Soar I**
- **Reverse Scale**
- **Inferno Emperor's Realm-Breaking Halberd**

### Practical advice
- Use a map you already understand.
- Decide whether you are going for a first kill or for efficient farming.
- Clean the boss pool if you want a serious Dragon King route.

[warn] If the Dragon Descendant still feels unstable for you, the Dragon King should not be your default farm target yet.
