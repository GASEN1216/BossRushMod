## Configuration

### Main configuration areas
- **Wave interval**
- **Randomized boss loot**
- **Use interact point between waves**
- **Lootbox bullet blocking**
- **Infinite Hell bosses per wave**
- **Global boss stat multiplier**
- **Mode D enemies per wave**
- **Dragon Set dash toggle**
- **Achievement hotkey**
- **Wild Horn wolf-model toggle**

### Important persisted data beyond sliders
- **Disabled boss list**
- **Infinite Hell boss factors**

### Recommended starting values
- Keep boss stat multiplier at `1`.
- Do not raise Infinite Hell boss count too quickly.
- Keep wave pacing readable until you really understand the systems.

[warn] Raising multiple pressure settings at once usually makes the mod harder to learn, not more enjoyable.
