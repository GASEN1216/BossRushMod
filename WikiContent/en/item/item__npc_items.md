## NPC Related Items

### Dingdang line
- **Diamond** for strong positive interaction.
- **Brick Stone** for a clearly negative reaction.
- **Dingdang Drawing** as a special gift-side item.

### Yu Zhi line
- **Peace Charm**
- **Calming Drops**

### Marriage line
- **Diamond Ring** is the proposal item.
- It only works properly when affinity and chapel requirements are already satisfied.

[tip] Before selling an unfamiliar BossRush item, ask whether it belongs to an NPC system line first.
