## Special and Mode Items

### Wild Horn
- A standout special item with its own strong identity.
- It is more about special experience than standard healing or damage utility.
- Configuration can switch it between wolf model usage and the original mount model route.

### Birthday Cake
- A more playful and thematic special item.
- Not core progression, but part of the mod's personality.

### Faction Flags
- Flags are the real entry keys for **Mode E**.
- Current flags include:
- **Random Faction Flag**
- **Scav Faction Flag**
- **USEC Faction Flag**
- **BEAR Faction Flag**
- **Lab Faction Flag**
- **Wolf Faction Flag**
- **Lone Wolf Flag**

### Mode E battlefield items
- **Taunt Smoke**
- **Chaos Detonator**
- **Bosscall Whistle**
- **Bloodhunt Beacon**

[warn] Mode E tools are powerful. If you use them carelessly, you can lose battlefield control immediately.
