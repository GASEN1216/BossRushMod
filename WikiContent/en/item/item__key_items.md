## Key Utility Items

### Boss Rush Ticket
- The basic entry item for standard BossRush.
- It also matters for **Mode D**, which requires **naked entry plus ticket**.

### Adventurer's Journal
- This is the in-game wiki book.
- It lets players check major systems without leaving the game.

### Cold Quench Fluid
- A core reforging material.
- Its main purpose is **locking stats** during reforge work.

### Achievement Medal
- A key item tied to long-term achievement progression.

[tip] These items may not look flashy, but they often gate whether you can access the mod's deeper systems at all.
