# Affinity and Marriage

# Affinity System

Dingdang and Yuori have a full Affinity system with 10 levels. Raising Affinity unlocks new services, discounts, and story content.

# Levels and Required Points

- Lv.1: 0 (initial)
- Lv.2: cumulative 50 (this level 50)
- Lv.3: cumulative 150 (this level 100)
- Lv.4: cumulative 300 (this level 150)
- Lv.5: cumulative 500 (this level 200)
- Lv.6: cumulative 750 (this level 250)
- Lv.7: cumulative 1,050 (this level 300)
- Lv.8: cumulative 1,400 (this level 350)
- Lv.9: cumulative 1,800 (this level 400)
- Lv.10: cumulative 2,300 (this level 500)

# Ways to Gain Affinity

- Daily chat: Dingdang +40 / Yuori +30 (once per day)
- Give a liked gift: +80 (once per day)
- Give a neutral gift: +20 (once per day)
- Give a disliked gift: -40 (Yuori receives -60 for Brick Stone) (once per day)
- Give Diamond Ring: +500
- Give Birthday Cake: +150

# Affinity Decay

If you do not interact with an NPC (chat or gift) on a given day, Affinity will drop by -15 the next day. Maintaining daily interaction prevents decay.

# Unlocks per Level

See the individual pages for Dingdang and Yuori for details.

# Marriage System

# Proposal Requirements

- Reach Affinity Lv.10 with the target NPC
- Build the Wedding Chapel (the building must be placed first)
- Give the NPC a Diamond Ring

# Wedding Sequence

- After giving the Diamond Ring, the NPC accepts the proposal
- A full-screen wedding cutscene plays (if the animation file is missing, a dialogue sequence is shown instead)
- A heart bubble and anniversary date appear above the NPC's head
- The NPC relocates to the Wedding Chapel

# Post-Marriage Changes

- NPC location: Moves to the Wedding Chapel
- Dialogue: Switches to marriage-exclusive dialogue
- New spouse interactions: you can ask your spouse to `Follow` from the chapel, and send them `Home` again while they are following
- Follow state is restored across common scene changes and returns to base whenever possible
- If a following spouse's Affinity falls below Lv.10, they automatically go home and the game shows a clear prompt
- Dingdang special perk: Gives Cold Quench Fluid during daily chat
- Yuori special perk: Gives Calming Drops during daily chat
- Replay wedding: The wedding cutscene can be replayed at the chapel

# Marriage Rules

- Monogamy: You can only be married to one NPC at a time
- Repeat proposal: Giving a ring to your spouse again while married will be refused; the ring is not consumed
- Cheating penalty: Giving a ring to another NPC while married consumes the ring, decreases Affinity by 120 points, and your spouse records the cheating event and scolds you during the next chat (each subsequent offense incurs an additional 40 point penalty)

# Divorce

- Interact with your spouse at the Wedding Chapel and select "Divorce"
- After divorce, Affinity resets to zero
- The NPC moves back to their original location
