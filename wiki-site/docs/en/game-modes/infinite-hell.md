# Infinite Hell

# Overview
- Infinite Hell is an endless survival variant of Standard BossRush.
- Bosses grow stronger every wave, there is no end, no evacuation — how many waves can you survive?

# Entry Requirements
- Have a **BossRush Ticket**.
- Select "Infinite Hell" difficulty at the arena signpost.

# Core Rules
- Bosses per wave: Default 3 (adjustable in config).
- Wave limit: Unlimited.
- Boss scaling: +2% health and damage per wave (cumulative).
- Loot crates: Do not drop.
- Economy system: Cash pool accumulates from Boss kills, displayed on the signpost.
- Clear condition: Cannot be cleared, survive until death.

# Cash Pool & Rewards
- Infinite Hell does not drop loot crates; instead it uses a cash pool system.
- Each Boss kill adds its value to the cash pool.
- The cash pool total is displayed on the signpost.
- Cash pickups within 2 meters on the field will automatically fly toward you.

# Milestone Rewards
- Every 5 waves: 1 high-quality random item (quality ≥ 5, value ≥ 10000).
- Every 100 waves: Milestone grand prize: Crown(s) + large cash reward (amount grows exponentially with each milestone).

# 100-Wave Milestone Rewards (Exponential Growth)
- 1st time (Wave 100): 1 Crown, 10 million cash.
- 2nd time (Wave 200): 2 Crowns, 20 million cash.
- 3rd time (Wave 300): 4 Crowns, 40 million cash.
- 4th time (Wave 400): 8 Crowns, 80 million cash.
- And so on: Crowns ×2, Cash ×2.

# Signpost Features
- In Infinite Hell mode, the signpost additionally provides:
- Ammo resupply shop.
- Current cash pool total display.
- Current wave display.

# Related Achievements
- Infinite Hell · Ten — Survive 10 waves (bonus 50,000).
- Infinite Hell · Twenty-Five — Survive 25 waves (bonus 150,000).
- Infinite Hell · Fifty — Survive 50 waves (bonus 350,000).
- Infinite Hell · Hundred — Survive 100 waves (bonus 800,000).
- Infinite Hell · Two Hundred — Survive 200 waves (bonus 2,000,000).
- Iron Will — 10 waves without taking damage (bonus 500,000).
- Iron Man Challenge (Hidden) — 10 waves without using any healing (bonus 250,000).

# Strategy Tips
- Boss stats increase by +2% per wave cumulatively, which becomes terrifying in later waves. Maintain an efficient kill pace early on.
- No loot crates means you need to bring enough gear into the arena, or rely on milestone rewards.
- Use the Boss Filter to adjust Boss pool weights to optimize your survival strategy.
