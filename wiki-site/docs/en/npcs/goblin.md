# Dingdang (Goblin Smith)

# Overview

Dingdang is a goblin created by J-Lab, possessing intelligence far beyond others of his kind. He is the core NPC of the equipment progression line in BossRush Mod, providing Reforge services and an exclusive shop.

# Services
- Chat (initial): Daily chat increases Affinity (+40/day)
- Gift (initial): You can give one gift per day
- Shop (Affinity Lv.2): Sells Diamond, Cold Quench Fluid, etc. More items and discounts unlock as Affinity increases
- Reforge (initial): Equipment attribute Reforge service

# Affinity Level Rewards
- Lv.2: Shop opens; receive Diamond
- Lv.3: 10% shop discount
- Lv.4: Receive Cold Quench Fluid
- Lv.5: Story dialogue (Dingdang's Past - Part 1)
- Lv.6: 15% shop discount
- Lv.7: Shop unlocks Diamond Ring
- Lv.10: Story dialogue (Dingdang's Past - Part 2); 20% shop discount; receive Dingdang's Drawing

# Gift Preferences
- Likes: Diamond, Birthday Cake, recipe/blueprint items (+80)
- Loves: Diamond Ring (+500)
- Neutral: Other items (+20)
- Dislikes: Brick Stone (fake diamond) (-40)

::: warning
If you give Dingdang's Drawing back to him, Affinity will drop by -300. This painting means a great deal to him.
:::

# Reforge Service

Dingdang's Reforge service is the core of equipment progression. See the Reforge system for details.

Reforge costs benefit from Affinity discounts: 10% off at Lv.3, 15% off at Lv.6, 20% off at Lv.10.

# Marriage

After reaching Affinity Lv.10, giving a Diamond Ring allows you to marry Dingdang. After marriage, Dingdang moves to the Wedding Chapel and gives Cold Quench Fluid as a bonus during daily chat.

See Affinity and Marriage for details.
