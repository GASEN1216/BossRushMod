# Awen (Courier)

# Overview

Awen is a Courier NPC who provides item storage and retrieval services. He appears in the base scene and also within Faction War and Blood Hunt modes, allowing you to store and retrieve items at any time during combat.

# Services
- Item Storage: Store items into a Storage Point, saved across scenes
- Item Retrieval: Retrieve previously stored items from the Storage Point

# Storage Point Mechanics
- Storage Point data is persistently saved and will not be lost when exiting the game
- Stored items can be retrieved in any scene where Awen appears
- The Storage Point serves as a bridge for transferring items between different modes

# Integration with Blood Hunt

When you successfully extract in Blood Hunt mode, bounty reward items are automatically sent to the Storage Point. Return to base and find Awen to retrieve these rewards after extraction.

# Tips
- Awen has no Affinity system and does not require daily interaction; just find him whenever you need to store or retrieve items
- Before entering high-risk modes, you can store valuable items with Awen to avoid accidental loss
- Blood Hunt extraction rewards are automatically sent to the Storage Point, so remember to pick them up after extraction
- Awen is present within both Faction War and Blood Hunt modes, so you can store and retrieve equipment at any time
