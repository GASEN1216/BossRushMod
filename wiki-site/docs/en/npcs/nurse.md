# Yuori (Nurse)

# Overview

Yuori is a former senior medical researcher at J-Lab. After the lab collapsed, she escaped to Duckov and opened a field clinic. She provides healing services and has her own Affinity and marriage storyline.

# Services
- Chat (initial): Daily chat increases Affinity (+30/day)
- Gift (initial): You can give one gift per day
- Healing (initial): Restores HP and removes negative Buffs; cost decreases with Affinity

# Affinity Level Rewards
- Lv.2: 10% healing discount
- Lv.3: Receive Calming Drops ×5
- Lv.4: 20% healing discount
- Lv.5: Story dialogue (Yuori's Memories)
- Lv.6: 25% healing discount
- Lv.7: 30% healing discount
- Lv.8: Receive Peace Amulet; heart bubble appears during chat
- Lv.9: 40% healing discount
- Lv.10: Story dialogue (Yuori's Confession)

# Gift Preferences
- Likes: Diamond, Birthday Cake, consumable/medical items (+80)
- Loves: Diamond Ring (+500)
- Neutral: Other items (+20)
- Dislikes: Brick Stone (reminds her of the lab) (-60)

# Marriage

After reaching Affinity Lv.10, giving a Diamond Ring allows you to marry Yuori. After marriage, Yuori moves to the Wedding Chapel and her dialogue switches to a marriage-exclusive version.

See Affinity and Marriage for details.

# Tips
- Yuori's healing discount can ultimately reach 40%, making long-term interaction very rewarding
- Calming Drops can remove most negative Buffs, very useful in high-difficulty modes
- Peace Amulet is a passive life-saving item that takes effect just by being in your inventory
