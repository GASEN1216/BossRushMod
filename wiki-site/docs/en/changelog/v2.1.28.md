# v2.1.28

# Release Date
- 2026-03-27

# Main Theme
- **Death Wraith System Added**: after a player dies, the game now leaves behind a Wraith record that inherits the player's look, gear, and combat style. Returning to the same map and sub-scene turns that death into a rematch.

# Detailed Update Log
- Added the Death Wraith system.
- When a player dies in a normal gameplay scene, the game records the death location, appearance, equipment, audio profile, and weapon state.
- Re-entering the same map and sub-scene will spawn the Wraith at the original death spot.
- The Wraith is classified into Strong, Balanced, or Weak tiers based on how much of your total wealth was tied up in the gear you were carrying at death.
- Each tier now has its own health, damage, and movement profile; even Weak Wraiths now get a small speed bonus.
- The Wraith automatically swaps between a firearm-style template and a wolf-style melee template depending on the loadout it brings back.
- The Wraith no longer hard-locks onto the player the moment the map loads. It now starts fighting after normal enemy-style target detection.
- The Wraith shows its own name and health bar, and defeating it clears that record.
- The Wraith itself does not drop a loot crate.
